Planet Earth: 2050

Earth in the future will look much different from know. One might even say it resembles a dystopian move.

Heatwaves

 With rising temperatures, the world will be plagued with heatwaves, deeming already hot locations uninhabitable.

Fire

 With dry forests, high temperatures, and chaotic weather massive wildfires will dominate the landscape.

Drought

 Freshwater will evaporate vigorously into the atmosphere, causing record long droughts.

Flooding

 With severe weather and rising sea levels, flash floods will become a normal occurrence.

Severe storms

 With warmer temperatures and more water in the atmosphere, great storms will materialize, creating hurricanes that stretch across countries.

Rising sea levels

 Due to ice melt and warmer seas, coastal cities such as those in Florida, Louisiana, California, New York and New Jersey will be submerged under the ocean.

Loss of biodiversity

 Rising sea levels, record high temperatures, and increased disease will kill off millions of species

Famine

 Neccesary conditions for farming will be scarce, causing global famine and unrest.

Poverty and displacement

 Millions of people will be displaced, fleeing from locations rendered uninhabitable. Unlucky souls will lose their livelyhoods to fire, floods, droughts, and storms.
